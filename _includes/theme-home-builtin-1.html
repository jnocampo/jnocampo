Description

Manoa Hungry Helper is a full-stack web application made for the hungry student/faculty/etc member on campus at the Univeristy of Hawai'i.

The application keeps track of all food vendors on campus.

The application allows users to search for specific meals, allowing filtering for dietary restrictions, and provide what is available to you on any given day. Vendors may also create accounts to update their shop's hours, menu, and location.

Visit Manoa Hungry Helper's homepage.
